Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is typically 21 years old. However, age requirements may vary by location and vehicle type. Additional fees may apply for renters under 25.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit or debit card, and proof of insurance if required. International renters may need a valid passport and an international driving permit.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation online or by contacting our customer service. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the time of cancellation.

Is there a mileage limit?

Most rentals come with unlimited mileage, but some vehicle types or rental locations may have mileage restrictions. Please check the specific terms and conditions of your rental agreement.

What should I do in case of an accident?

In case of an accident, ensure everyone's safety first. Contact the local authorities and then notify our rental office. You will need to fill out an accident report form and provide relevant details and documentation.
